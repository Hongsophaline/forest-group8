We make it possible for
Anyone to do good


We are all the network


We can’t do this work alone, so we never do. Our
network is our members supporting planting projects
around the world and in their own back yards. Our
corporate partners giving back to the planet. Cities
and campuses committing to community forestry. And
our planting partners with their hands in the dirt. No
matter who you are, there’s a place for you in this
community that’s shaping the future through trees. 


How support becomes action


Our more than 50 years of experience has
allowed us to develop relationships with planting
organizations around the world who are doing it
right. They know their local communities and
regions deeply. The magic happens when we
connect their local knowledge with the support,
guidance, and resources, so the trees we plant
can grow the greatest impact for all of us. 


Turning bold ideas into action


Trees have the power to transform many worlds,
from front yards to recovering forests. Whatever
the ambition, we’re ready to activate. Whether it’s a
local utility company distributing trees to shade
customers’ homes from summer heat or planting
millions of trees online with #TeamTrees, the way
projects come to life can look as different as the
projects themselves. With those bold enough to
imagine innovative projects, we’re ready to make
them a reality. 


Transparency
