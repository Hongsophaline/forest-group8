Forests are our
greatest hope

We joined forces to urgently speed up and scale
up the positive power of forests. Together,
we are committed to protect and restore
one trillion trees, for the benefit of people,
nature and a stable climate.



Why forests?

We are facing three of the biggest global emergencies
in the history of the human race. Forests sequester
more carbon, regulate the climate, sustain biodiversity
and provide food and livelihoods for millions.
We need impact at scale and fast - forests are
our greatest hope.
